About Blog Now

Hi, I'm Nox. Born in California, grew up in Germany.

As a teenager I fell hard into Jedi Knight 2 multiplayer. Lightsaber combat, force powers, Quake 3 physics, and a small community of hardcore players.

I made lifelong friends there.

When Ravensoft released the source code, Daggolin taught me C. My first code was small clientside mods: tweaking the UX.


I didn't take programming seriously until 2020. Now I'm in San Francisco.
